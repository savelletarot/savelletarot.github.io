Nice to Meet You

My journey with Tarot began a decade ago. It helped illuminate paths and perspectives that helped guide my direction to a better life. I still had to do the work to implement those changes. But, the messages I received from Tarot readings definitely had an impact on that growth.

I grew up on the Navajo reservation in southwest America. My father was a pastor. Growing up, he instilled a deep respect for all things spiritual. I experienced many unexplainable events and situations. Over time, my relationship with my intuition grew. The messages come in all shapes and forms, including lucid dreams, coincidences, revelations and now Tarot readings.

I enjoy pulling Tarot cards with intent and care. Especially when they help others who may feel lost or confused. I believe that we all know the answer to our questions. But, Tarot can help provide a unique perspective on the "answer" in a way that helps you accept it. If you have a question, I'd love to pull cards for you and see what comes up!

-Savelle
